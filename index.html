Step-by-Step Guide

1

Remove Cup Rim

Cut off the top rim and set it aside for the handle.

2

Cut Vertical Sections

Slice the cup vertically into 7 equal flaps from top to base, without cutting through the base.

3

Spread Flaps Out

Gently bend the flaps outward like flower petals.

4

Weave the Rope

Weave the rope over and under the flaps in a circular motion until you reach the top.

5

Trim & Finish

Cut any extra rope and press the weave to secure it in place.

6

Attach Handle

Bend the removed rim into an arch and insert both ends into the basket sides as a handle.
